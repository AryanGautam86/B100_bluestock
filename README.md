# Bluestock Fundamental Analysis Dashboard

##  Project Overview

The Bluestock Fundamental Analysis Dashboard is a data analytics project designed to analyze the financial performance of leading Indian companies across multiple sectors. The project transforms raw financial statement data into meaningful insights through data cleaning, exploratory data analysis (EDA), financial ratio evaluation, and interactive Power BI visualizations.

The primary objective is to help investors, analysts, and stakeholders understand company performance, profitability trends, revenue growth, and overall financial health through a data-driven approach.

---

##  Objectives

- Clean and preprocess raw financial datasets.
- Perform Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA).
- Analyze revenue, profit, and financial performance trends.
- Calculate important financial metrics and ratios.
- Develop a composite Company Health Score.
- Create interactive Power BI dashboards for business insights.
- Support informed investment and business decisions.

#  Key Insights

## Revenue Analysis

- Energy sector generated the highest overall revenue.
- Banking sector ranked second in revenue contribution.
- Revenue across the dataset showed strong growth from 2013 to 2024.

## Company Performance

- Reliance Industries emerged as the top revenue-generating company.
- Reliance Industries also recorded the highest net profit.
- TCS and Infosys demonstrated strong profitability and financial stability.

## Financial Health Analysis

A composite Company Health Score was developed using:

- Profitability Metrics
- Cash Flow Performance
- Debt Management
- Interest Coverage Ratio

Companies with strong profitability and healthy cash flows achieved higher health scores.

## Sector-Level Observations

- Technology companies exhibited stable profitability and strong financial health.
- Banking and insurance firms naturally showed higher Debt-to-Equity ratios due to industry structure.
- Energy and infrastructure sectors contributed significantly to total market revenue.

##  Dashboard Features

The Power BI dashboard enables users to:

- Compare company revenues and profits.
- Analyze sector-wise performance.
- Track company health scores.
- Identify top-performing companies.
- Explore revenue growth trends over time.
- Gain interactive business insights through visual analytics.

##  Business Impact

This project demonstrates how financial statement data can be transformed into actionable insights using data analytics and visualization techniques. The dashboard helps stakeholders evaluate company performance, identify growth opportunities, and support data-driven decision-making.
